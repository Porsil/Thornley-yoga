Classes

Power Yoga

60 minutes of Ashtanga and Vinyasa yoga. Faster paced styles which are more challenging and physically demanding. Designed to work up a sweat and burn calories and helps to detox the body.

Stretch Yoga

60 minutes of Hatha and Iyengar yoga. Slower paced styles focused on balance, stretching and posture. Improves posture and increases flexibility whilst toning and strengthening the whole body.

Restorative Yoga

40 minutes of slow-paced yoga with gentle stretches followed by 20 minutes of guided meditation. Ideal for relaxing the mind and relieving stress and tension from the mind and body.
